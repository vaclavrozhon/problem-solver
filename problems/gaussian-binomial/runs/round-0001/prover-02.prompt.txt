# Periodicity at primitive $n$-th roots

Let $\{f_n(q)\}_{n\ge 0}$ be defined by

$$
f_n(q)\;=\;\sum_{i=0}^{n} \qbinom{n+i}{2i},
$$

where the symmetrized Gaussian $q$-binomial coefficients are defined via

$$
[n]\;=\;\frac{v^{\,n}-v^{-n}}{v-v^{-1}},\qquad
\qbinom{n}{k}\;=\;\frac{[n][n-1]\cdots[n-k+1]}{[k][k-1]\cdots[1]},
$$

and $q=v^2$.

**Claim.** The sequence $\bigl(f_n\!\big(e^{2\pi i/n}\big)\bigr)_{n\ge 0}$ is periodic in $n$.
